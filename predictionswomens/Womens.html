WOMENS

    The women fights may not always be the most entertaining, but there are plently of intriguing match-ups to keep an eye on for over the next several months. The most anticipating one of them all.. Nunes vs. Shevchenko. You will not find a closer fight than that..

    • UFC Fight Night: June 17th - Holly Holm vs. Bethe Correia

    • Holly Holm needs to get back on track and what better way to do so other than a fight against Bethe Correia. Her recklessness and ego is tailer made for Holly to take advantage of.

      Prediction: Holm via 49-46 UD.

    • UFC Fight Night: June 25th - Felice Herrig vs. Justine Kish

    • A veteran vs. an undefeated prospect. Kish is good, but she hasn't been with someone like Felice yet..

      Prediction: Herrig via round 2 submission (Rear-Naked-Choke).

    • UFC 213: July 08 - Amanda Nunes vs. Valentina Shevchenko

    • Basically the Daniel Cormier vs. Jon Jones match-up of the womans division. This fight is a toss-up. Shevchenko is deadly everywhere, but Nunes could be on another level when it comes to the stand-up. Over the course of a 5 round fight, you got to believe that the more tools you have, the better the chances of victory..

      Prediction: Shevchenko via 48-47 SD.

    • UFC Fight Night: July 16 - Joanne Calderwood vs. Cynthia Cavillo

    • A huge step up in competition for Cynthia. Calderwood only loses to elite fighters, but is Cavillo an elite fighter? I don't think so.

      Prediction: Calderwood via 29-28 UD.

    • UFC 214: July 29 - Kailin Curran vs. Alexandra Albu

    • Albu is an intriguing young prospect with a ton of untapped potential. Curran is a decent fighter, but she is not really dangerous in any particular area. Look for Alexandra to dominant from the get-go and secure an early victory.

      Prediction: Albu via first round submission (Rear-Naked-Choke).
